Total Score is the weighted mean  s(x) = Σw·si / Σw  (eq 1), computed from your components as given — REINVENT's convention is that each component is already a transformed score in [0,1] where higher is better. The direction toggles affect only the Pareto dominance test (maximize objectives are sign-inverted internally, exactly as the notebook prescribes).

Pareto test is O(N²); ranking first keeps it fast.
